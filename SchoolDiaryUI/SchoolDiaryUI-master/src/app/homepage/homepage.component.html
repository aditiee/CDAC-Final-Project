    Welcome to School Diary

    School eDiary is an advanced application for school to make better communication between student/parent and teacher.Relevant features like Notice Board, Class Diary, Profile, Attendance,Academic Details,etc has been packed in the application in an easy to use interface.

    About Us

    School eDiary is a light application with clean UI designed to replace the paper-based school diary. We are trying to simplify the commnication process between school and students or parents in a paperless way. In today's world almost every parent has a Smartphone, where they find and fill theirbasic need. So we are trying to add their childs's academic need and performance in a single application.

    24/7 availability

    The well-functioning of the e-diary is in the hands of a team of professionals, who are in charge not only of proper performance of the software, but also of the security of the information stored therein.

    Information security

    E-diary is available to students, parents and teachers all day, every day. A click on the link on the homepage opens the portal which you access with your username and password, assigned to you by the school.

    01

    Administration

    Admin has all the records of student, parent & faculty. Communicate with parents regulatory bodies and the public.

    02

    Faculty

    Faculty here can mark attendance, add notice class wise, update result of students. This helps faculty to keep a track of the students in his/her respective class.

    03

    Parents

    Parents are able to view attendance and result of his/her child. This helps them to keep a track of their child's progress.

    04

    Students

    Students here can view their profile, attendance, class notice, result.

    Services

    Notice Board

    Instance alerts on any circular/notice from school.Students can view all the global and class notices.

    Profile

    Displays complete profile of the student, parent details, class teacher details, addresss and emergency contact number.

    Attendance

    Detailed view of the student attendance in the current academic year including presents, absents and leaves.

    Result

    Parents can see the term-wise and subject-wise marks statement of their ward thereby keeping a track of their academic performance.

    Feedback

    Parents/Teachers can share their valuable feedback about school infrastructure or curriculum to the admin.

    Contact Us

    Remain updated about the contact details, website,social media accounts and visiting hours of the schools from the app.
